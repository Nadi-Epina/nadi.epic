User testing

I have conducted 5 user interviews. I was specifically looking for people in FB chats who are looking for housing for a month, or a week, or a vacation. I invited them to an interview, asked them what exactly they see, how they understand the project, what they don't like or like. It was very important to find non-loyal customers who could pass the security test.

Key Findings

  • Perceived Purpose:
    Respondents initially believe the service is more about community building and finding friends rather than renting accommodation.

  • Feature Requests:
    Users want clearer details on trial benefits and usage.

  • Post-Registration:
    After registering, users were frustrated by unclear next steps.

  • Cost Comparison:
    Initial impressions suggest that staying at a hotel might be cheaper than using LinkedLivin.

  • Security Approval Delay:
    Users were not informed that their files were sent to security for approval, which could take up to 36 hours.

  • Service Pricing:
    The cost of the service is unclear, leading to confusion.
