Blue Bear School of music. Non-profit Organization.

Blue Bear School of Music was founded as “Blue Bear Waltzes School of Genuine Music” in the summer of 1971 by a rock band called “Wolfgang & Strauss.”

As a nonprofit organization, Blue Bear partners with schools, shelters, and community centers to bring the transformative power of playing music to underserved populations for FREE.

Company Motto: We believe making class prices and services more available for parents and people who want to learn music will achieve more enrollment for Blue Bear School of Music.

User Survey

How might we improve the website’s listed services so that Blue Bear School of Music’s customers are successfully able to enroll?

Process
Created a 5 questions Google Survey

Responses
Collected 16 responses

Key Findings
Class/program offerings are unclear Navigation is confusing Users liked current site graphics

Developing Solutions

Site map

I changed the location of class price and discounts information, and the data about discounts and offers. I also renamed the name of the groups and adjusted the discount process.

Paper Prototypes

We started by creating rough paper prototypes to collaborate on with team.

Wareframing-flows

Then transitioned to wireframes to ensure our design addressed our original focus area of improved navigation.

Usability Testing

There were many changes and discussions about the design after, a drop menu was introduced, and the understanding of how to get a discount or scale price was changed. the design of the home page and the navigation menu have been changed. The stakeholder really wanted to keep the color palette

Process
Defined 3 tasks for testers:
1. Sign up 3yr old
2. Sign up for an adult guitar lesson
3. Locating pricing info

Responses
7 people who were visiting the site for the first time

Key Findings
Cards were helpful for navigation Little Bears schedule confused users Adult class sign-up section was misleading

Results

Sign up 3yr old for class

Sign up for adult program

Find Pricing Page

Final thougts

Blue Bear School of Music is a respected organization in the Bay Area community. The site already reflected their character, we just focused on improving navigation so more people would complete the signup process.
Was helpful to get feedback from the one of their program directors on their focus areas to help define scope and avoid scope creep, especially with the constrained timeline.

Future development

We recommend refining the Community section of the site as many of the pages are outdated and difficult to navigate.
We did not include this in our initial scope as the sign-up process was our main focus.
