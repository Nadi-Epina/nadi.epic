Competitive Analysis

My goal was to study competitors in fashion industry, focusing on Japanese style, to understand their strengths, weaknesses, and innovative features. I am researching the search and filtering functions of competitors to identify best practices.

SUMMARY

  • Reputation and Stylish Design: Competitors like Style-arena and Droptokyo.com are known for their reputation and stylish design, which could attract users.
  • Simplicity of Navigation: Some competitors, such as Instagram, offer simple navigation, which can be appealing to users looking for ease of use.
  • Content Limitations: Platforms like Instagram and Pinterest have content limitations due to algorithms, potentially leading to less diverse fashion content.
  • Access to Content: Vogue restricts access to its best content behind a paywall, which could be a barrier for users.
  • Based on these insights, Tokyo Fashion App could develop a strategy to improve its search and filter functionality, taking into account the strengths and weaknesses of its competitors.
